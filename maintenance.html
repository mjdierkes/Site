HVAC Maintenance Professionals Proudly Serving Arlington, Grand Prairie, Mansfield, Kennedale, TX & Surrounding Areas

Regular maintenance is a must to keep your HVAC systems running properly. Air Techs Heating & Cooling offers a variety of maintenance services and plans that can help you keep your furnace and air conditioning systems operating at optimal efficiency to save you money and provide absolute comfort indoors. Our team of skilled service technicians can create a maintenance regimen that is precisely right for your needs.

Protecting Your Investment With an HVAC Maintenance Plan

HVAC experts agree that regular maintenance visits are essential to extend the life and protect the performance of your heating and cooling system At Air Techs Heating & Cooling, we offer furnace and AC maintenance plans that can prepare your system for the chill of winter and the heat of summer to help you enjoy superior comfort all year around. Our service technicians perform a number of essential tasks that can keep your heating and cooling system in tip-top shape:

  • Inspecting your system to identify any areas of concern
  • Cleaning coils, drain lines, and other parts of your HVAC system
  • Replacing filters and checking pulleys and belts for signs of wear
  • Changing batteries in your thermostat or other components of your system
  • Testing thermostats and measuring humidity levels
  • Checking charge and levels of refrigerant
  • Identifying any areas of concern with the performance of your air conditioning or heating systems
  • Lubricating fan motors and checking on their operation
  • Looking for leaks and other signs of HVAC issues

Your Furnace and AC Maintenance Schedule

Your Air Techs Heating & Cooling maintenance plan usually includes two visits each year. In the fall, we will perform maintenance on your furnace to get it ready to take on the winter's chill. Each spring, we will return to perform AC maintenance that can help your cooling system manage even the hottest temperatures in Arlington, Kennedale, Grand Prairie, Mansfield, Fort Worth, TX, and the surrounding areas. By investing in these twice-yearly HVAC maintenance visits, you can significantly extend the life of your system and can reduce the risk of unexpected breakdowns for the future of your HVAC system.
